Mamata Banerjee: After verdict for Singur's farmers, I can die in peace

Shailaja Neelakantan | Aug 31, 2016, 03.59 PM IST

NEW DELHI: West Bengal chief minister Mamata Banerjee, who first came to power riding the wave of the protests against the Singur land deal, said on Wednesday that she can die in peace after the Supreme Court quashed+ the land acquisition.
